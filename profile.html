About Us

“Sorry hottie I’m not into guys now, I am more into myself. Don’t fall in love with me”

An excerpt from Svmmerdose’s latest song “Crush” which could be argued as a contradictory line in such a world driven by love, lust, attention and desire. these young, passion-driven with bullet-speed dream lads write about what the world need to hear in a full honesty—accentuated with catchy hooks and stumping beats, the band is an inevitable trio to listen to.

Svmmerdose consists of Tarrarin (Vocals), Abbydzar (Guitar), and Iqbaal (Bass). With the genre Alternative R&B experimental, their songs have taken them to a numerous stage and festivals including Lalala festival alongside with The Internet, Honne, Gentle Bones, and many more. They also have twice gotten the recognition from Indonesian AMI awards as Next Big Things in Indonesia.

Started from various skype calls in 2016 all the way from Montezuma, USA to Bogor, Indonesia, the three of them decided to put their thoughts out in songs under the band’s name. Their debut single ‘Independent' offers catchy bass line, experimental-sounded guitar licks, a bit touch of alternative ambience, combined with Tarrarin’s voice in which strikingly hit the feels, making the song as a complete tune that has successfully stolen a vast of audience.

Not only an irresistibly-dancing type of music the band is playing, their shows also offers eye-catching visuals and lighting shows as complimentary parts of the show for the intention of the band is to visually and audibly please the crowd. Svmmerdose, by far, has released 3 singles: Independent, Got it all, Crush and are working on an EP that will be out this year.
